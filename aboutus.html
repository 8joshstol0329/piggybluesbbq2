Our History & Passion

Since 2002, Piggy Blues BBQ has been Austin, MN's go-to spot for authentic, slow-smoked barbecue. Founded by Ron Meyer, a seasoned butcher and competitive BBQ enthusiast, our restaurant combines generations of passion with the freshest local ingredients to deliver mouthwatering flavors. Our team, led by pitmaster Josh Diaz, is dedicated to perfecting the art of barbecue through a meticulous 12-hour smoking process using hickory and oak wood. At Piggy Blues BBQ, we believe in tradition, quality, and community, serving up more than just food – we serve an experience that brings people together over great meals and warm hospitality.

From Butcher to Pitmaster

Ron Meyer, a fourth-generation butcher, realized his true calling wasn't just cutting meat, but slow-smoking it to perfection. He applies his meticulous knowledge of cuts and quality to every piece of brisket and every rack of ribs. His commitment to sourcing local, high-grade meat is the foundation of our flavor.

This hands-on, quality-first approach is why Piggy Blues has earned a reputation for being the best BBQ in Southern Minnesota for over two decades.

Tradition in Every Bite

Today, the tradition is carried on by Manager and Pitmaster Josh Diaz, who blends the original family recipes with his own passion for the craft. We exclusively use a mix of hickory and oak, smoking our meats for up to 12 hours to achieve that deep, authentic flavor that can't be rushed.

It's not just food; it's a family legacy of blues and barbecue blended in every bite. Come taste the difference that patience and passion make!
